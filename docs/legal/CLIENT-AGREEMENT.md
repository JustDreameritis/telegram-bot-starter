# Client Agreement

## 1. Scope of Work
The specific deliverables, timeline, and acceptance criteria are defined in the project's Statement of Work (SOW). This agreement governs the general terms under which all work is performed.

## 2. Payment Terms
- **Milestone-based billing**: payments are tied to defined deliverables, not hours
- **Standard split**: 50% upfront (project kickoff), 50% on final delivery
- **For projects over $500**: 3 milestones (30% / 40% / 30%)
- **Payment via**: Upwork escrow (preferred) or direct invoice (Net 7)
- **Late payment**: work pauses after 7 days overdue; no penalty for client, but timeline resets

## 3. Revision Policy
- **2 revision rounds** included in all Standard and Advanced packages
- A "revision" is a change within the agreed scope — not a new feature
- Revisions requested within 7 days of delivery are covered
- Additional revisions or scope changes billed at $50/hour

## 4. Intellectual Property
- **Client owns all final deliverables** — source code, documentation, configurations
- Ownership transfers upon final payment
- Developer retains the right to use **general techniques, patterns, and non-proprietary tools** developed during the project (but not client-specific business logic or data)
- Open-source dependencies remain under their respective licenses

## 5. Confidentiality
- All client data, credentials, business logic, and communications are treated as confidential
- No client information will be shared with third parties
- Code samples may be used in the developer's portfolio **only with explicit written client consent**
- API keys, passwords, and secrets are never stored in version control

## 6. Limitation of Liability
- Developer's total liability is limited to the amount paid for the project
- Developer is not liable for: third-party API outages, hosting provider failures, data loss due to client infrastructure, or losses from production deployment decisions made by the client
- Client is responsible for production deployment decisions and backups
- Developer provides best-effort support during the post-delivery support window

## 7. Termination
- Either party may terminate with 7 days written notice
- Client pays for all completed milestones and work-in-progress
- All code and documentation produced to date is delivered to the client upon termination

## 8. Communication
- Primary channel: Upwork messages (or agreed platform)
- Response time: within 24 hours on business days
- Progress updates: as defined in the SOW

---
*This agreement template is provided for reference. Final terms are confirmed in writing before project kickoff.*
